Latitude vs Wind Speed


Wind Speed's appear to largely stable across latitudes. The majority of cities experienced wind speed's somewhere between 0 and 15 mph. The significant exceptions, however, seem to be at the latitudinal extremes. The scatter shown above suggests that extreme wind speeds may be more common the farther you are from the equator, but more data would have to be analyzed to make any more comprehensive conclusions.
